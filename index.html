Privacy Policy for Bprod

Effective Date: October 27, 2025

Thank you for using Bprod - Be Productive ("the extension"). Your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what information the extension handles and, more importantly, what it *doesn't* do.

Our guiding philosophy is simple: Your data is your own. It never leaves your computer.

What Information We Collect

Bprod is designed to be your personal productivity partner. To provide its features, the extension handles two types of data:

  • Productivity Data: When you start a Pomodoro timer, the extension logs the website domain you are currently viewing (e.g., "google.com") and the amount of time spent there. This tracking only occurs when the timer is actively running. This data is used to populate your "Domain Breakdown" and calendar heatmap.
  • User-Entered Data: This includes the to-do list items you voluntarily add for each day ("Today's Tasks") and your personal settings for "Daily Goal" and "Pomodoro Time".

How Your Data is Stored

All information handled by Bprod (your domain history, task lists, and settings) is stored locally on your computer using the built-in chrome.storage.local API.

We do not have a server. Your data is never sent to any external server, analytics service, or database. We (the developers of Bprod) do not have access to your data. It stays entirely within your own Chrome browser.

How Your Data is Used

Your data is used only for the following features, which are visible only to you:

  • To display your "Total / Goal" and "vs Yesterday" stats.
  • To show your list of "Today's Tasks" in the popup and dashboard.
  • To render your productivity history in the "Domain Breakdown" list.
  • To populate the monthly calendar heatmap with your daily totals.
  • To generate a .csv file on your computer *only when you* click one of the "Export" buttons. This file is created on your device and is not sent anywhere.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third parties. Period. Since your data is only on your computer, we have no data to share.

Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete individual domain entries for the current day from the "Domain Breakdown" card in the dashboard.
  • You can delete individual tasks from the "Tasks" list in the popup or dashboard.
  • You can delete all of your stored data at any time by clicking the "Clear All Data" button in the dashboard's "Export / Import" section. This action is irreversible and permanently deletes your history from your computer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and/or on the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.
